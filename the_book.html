Music by Numbers: The Guide to Essential Piano Scales is our Main Product it has all of the essential scales you will need to learn piano and pass your grade exams.

 

A Different Type of Scales Book

 

Other scales books come grade by grade with ten to twenty scales in each book. Our book is much better value with over one hundred scales including Major, Minor Melodic, Minor Harmonic, Major Arpeggios, Minor Arpeggios, Chromatic Scales, Contrary Motion and Pentatonics.

What makes Music by Numbers different is that we dont use stave notation for the scales, we illustrate them visually using a diagram of a piano and the finger numbers.

The book is also spiral bound so that it sits well on a piano stand. It really is made by piano players for piano players.
