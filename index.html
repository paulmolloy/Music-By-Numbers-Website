Music By Numbers

Music By Numbers is a new visual way to learn your piano scales using a piano diagram and numbers. Now with this new system anyone can learn piano scales in minutes.

Each one of your fingers are assigned a number. On the diagram of the keyboard the numbers correspond to your fingers. You press the keys with the correct finger number and play all scales except contrary motion scales from left to right and then back again right to left.

We are a transition year mini company from Castleknock Community College, in Dublin.
